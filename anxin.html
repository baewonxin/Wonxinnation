Zhou Anxin

Anxin (안신) is a Chinese trainee and actor under CHROMOSOME (EXO LAY's agency) and Nouer Entertainment. He is a member of BOYS || PLANET debuting group ALPHA DRIVE ONE (ALD1).

"Why are you so cute?"

He was officially revealed as a contestant on Boys || Planet on the visual poster, attracting the eyes of many fans with his unique visuals. During the show's finale, he ranked 2nd overall, thus making him a member of ALPHA DRIVE ONE.
